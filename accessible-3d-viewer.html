Keyboard Shortcuts

Use these keyboard shortcuts from anywhere on the page:

  • 1 - Go shallower (decrease depth by 10%)
  • 2 - Go deeper (increase depth by 10%)
  • Q - Go shallower (decrease depth by 1%)
  • E - Go deeper (increase depth by 1%)
  • 4 - Zoom out
  • 5 - Zoom in
  • 7 - Switch to x- view
  • 8 - Switch to x+ view
  • 9 - Switch to z+ view
  • 0 - Switch to z- view
  • - - Switch to y- view
  • = - Switch to y+ view
  • F - Switch to filled/shaded view
  • O - Switch to outline view
  • C - Switch to cut view
  • R - Switch render mode (cycle through filled, outline, cut)
  • T - Switch view mode (cycle through single, side-by-side, and slice-graph)
  • L - Toggle slice graph lock
  • G - Refresh slice graph from current location
  • W - Move scene up
  • A - Move scene left
  • S - Move scene down
  • D - Move scene right
  • P - Print current render to renders/print_0.pdf
  • A - Get current status (view, depth, mode)
  • H - Shortcut reminder
  • Escape - Clear focus from current element

Note: These shortcuts are optimized for Braille displays and screen readers. They work globally without needing to focus on specific elements first.
